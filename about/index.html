About Me

Hi, I am Samantha-Rae Adamson

Overview

I am a dedicated and enthusastic teacher. Inclusive, supportive and diverse environments are the key to making successful learners. Over the years, I have had experience working with individuals of all ages and intellectual needs and it has helped me learn how to dedicate and prioritize my time to best suit the needs of the individual.

I have hobbies: I love reading, I paint quirky watercolour prints, I play slopitch softball (not well, but not the point), traveling and I like playing around with web design and open source graphic software.

Personal Story

Educational Philosophy

I am lucky that two of my passions - English literature and Computer Science - are what I consider the building blocks for many of our students futures because they help develop key skills and confidence that filters over into all aspects of their lives. Communication and adaptability are key components of successful learning and developing confidence. Teaching English doesn't have to be about exploring the story - but a catalyst for effective and meaningful communication. Technology is the future: our young people should have the skills and confidence in technology so that they feel relevant and competitive in any field they choose to pursue.
