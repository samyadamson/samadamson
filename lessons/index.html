Short Story Unit

A short story unit I created in collaboration with another teacher. Unit focuses on the idea of privilege through short stories and helps students write their own story while understanding how to recognize and discuss privilege in society.

Computer Science Best Practices

A handout outlining best practices for computer programming created for a beginner group of grade 9 or 10 using C#. Should be used as a reference sheet throughout the year, but especially as they first begin developing their skills and coding habits.
